Creamy Tomato Pasta

This creamy tomato pasta is quick, easy, and delicious for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients :

  • 200g pasta

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped

  • 1/2 cup cream

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instruction :

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions and drain.

  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.

  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.

  4. Stir in cream and mix well.

  5. Add pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.

  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with basil, and serve hot.

Chocolate Mug Cake

A quick microwave chocolate mug cake for instant dessert cravings.

Ingrdients :

  1. 4 tbsp flour

  2. 4 tbsp sugar

  3. 2 tbsp cocoa powder

  4. 3 tbsp milk

  5. 2 tbsp oil

  6. 1/4 tsp baking powder

  7. Chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions :

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a mug.

  2. Add milk and oil, mix until smooth.

  3. Stir in chocolate chips if using.

  4. Microwave for 1-2 minutes until cake rises.

  5. Let it cool slightly and enjoy!

Fun Fact :

This mug cake can be made in just 5 minutes!

Healthy Veggie Salad

A fresh vegetable salad to boost your health.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber

  • 2 tomatoes

  • 1 carrot

  • 1 bell pepper

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • Salt & pepper

Instructions :

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make dressing.

  3. Toss vegetables with dressing and serve fresh.
