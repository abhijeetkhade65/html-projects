About MVJ College

Our History

MVJ College started with a handful of disciplines and now offers a broad set of programs. The college emphasizes research, industry interface and student well-being.

Timeline (click to expand)
  1. 1982 — Founded
  2. 1995 — First postgraduate program.
  3. 2008 — Research center inaugurated.
  4. 2020 — New innovation lab opened
Mission
To educate and empower students with cutting-edge technical skills and ethical leadership.
Vision
To be a center of excellence in engineering education and research.
